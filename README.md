# RakshAI: AI-Powered Income Protection for Quick Commerce Delivery Partners

## 1. Problem Statement

Quick commerce delivery partners form the backbone of platforms such as Blinkit and Zepto. Their income is directly tied to active working hours. External disruptions such as heavy rainfall, extreme heat, and platform outages can immediately reduce their ability to complete deliveries.

These disruptions lead to unpredictable income loss within a single day. Existing insurance products are not aligned with the nature of gig work, which operates on short earning cycles and requires immediate support. There is a need for a system that protects income in real time and adapts to external conditions.

---

## 2. Target Persona

**Persona: Quick Commerce Delivery Partner (Blinkit / Zepto)**

- Works 8 to 10 hours per day  
- Operates within a 3 to 5 km delivery zone  
- Earnings depend on order availability and working conditions  

**Frequently affected by:**
- Weather conditions such as rain and heat  
- Sudden drops in demand  
- Platform or system disruptions  

The solution must be fast, simple, reliable and tailored to their unique situation.

---

## 3. Solution Overview

RakshAI is an AI-powered parametric insurance platform designed to protect delivery partners from income loss caused by environmental and digital disruptions. The platform predicts expected earnings, monitors real-time conditions, and automatically triggers payouts when income drops below a defined threshold due to verified disruptions. The system follows a weekly pricing model aligned with the earning patterns of gig workers and enables a seamless claims experience.

---

## 4. Core Features

| Feature | Description | Value Delivered |
|--------|------------|----------------|
| Income Gap Insurance | Predicts expected daily earnings using historical data and compares with actual earnings. Triggers payout when income drops beyond threshold due to verified disruptions. | Personalised income protection aligned with real earning patterns |
| Hyperlocal Zone Risk Engine | Assigns dynamic risk scores to 3–5 km delivery zones using rainfall, traffic, order density, and disruption history. | Fair and location-specific weekly premium calculation |
| Reliability Score | Evaluates rider behaviour using login consistency, delivery activity, GPS validation, and claim patterns. | Reduces fraud risk and rewards consistent workers with better pricing |
| Surge Protection | Predicts high-risk conditions and allows riders to opt for increased coverage for the upcoming period. | Enables proactive protection and user control |
| Platform Failure Detection | Monitors platform activity such as rider drop-offs and order volume to detect outages and disruptions. | Covers income loss from digital disruptions, not just environmental factors |
| RakshAI Dashboard | Displays risk levels, disruption forecasts, coverage status, and payout likelihood to the rider. | Builds transparency, trust, and informed decision-making |

---

### 4.1 Income Gap Insurance

RakshAI predicts expected daily earnings using historical activity patterns.  
Actual earnings are tracked and compared against this prediction. If a significant drop is detected due to a verified disruption, a payout is triggered automatically.

**Payout Logic:**
- Expected Earnings predicted from historical data  
- Actual Earnings tracked in real time  
- Income drop beyond threshold triggers compensation  

---

### 4.2 Hyperlocal Zone Risk Engine

The city is divided into small delivery zones. Each zone is assigned a dynamic risk score based on local conditions.

**Inputs considered:**
- Rainfall intensity  
- Traffic congestion  
- Order density trends  
- Historical disruptions  

The risk score directly influences the weekly premium for riders operating in that zone.

---

### 4.3 Reliability Score

Each delivery partner is assigned a reliability score based on behavioural patterns.

**Inputs include:**
- Login consistency  
- Delivery acceptance rate  
- GPS-based location validation  
- Claim history  

This score influences pricing and helps detect anomalous behaviour.

---

### 4.4 Surge Protection

RakshAI identifies high-risk conditions in advance and notifies the delivery partner.

The rider can opt for increased coverage for the upcoming period by paying a small additional amount.

This enables proactive risk management.

---

### 4.5 Platform Failure Detection

RakshAI monitors platform activity to detect system-level disruptions.

**Signals include:**
- Sudden drop in active riders  
- Order volume fluctuations  
- Platform status indicators  

Validated disruptions trigger automatic income protection.

---

### 4.6 RakshAI Dashboard

The platform provides a transparency layer for delivery partners.

**The dashboard displays:**
- Current risk levels  
- Disruption forecasts  
- Active coverage status  
- Estimated payout likelihood  

This builds trust and awareness.

---

## 5. Weekly Pricing Model

The pricing model aligns with weekly earning cycles.

**Base Premium:** ₹29 per week  

**Adjusted based on:**
- Zone Risk Score  
- Reliability Score  

**Illustrative Outcomes:**
- Lower risk and higher reliability result in reduced premium  
- Higher risk conditions increase premium  

**Coverage:**
- Triggered on income drop beyond threshold  
- Weekly payout capped to maintain sustainability  

---

## 6. Parametric Triggers

RakshAI uses predefined triggers to validate disruptions.

**Triggers include:**
- Rainfall above threshold levels  
- Extreme temperature conditions  
- Significant drop in order volume  
- Platform outages  

These enable automated and objective claim initiation.

---

## 7. AI and Data Integration

AI is integrated across key system components.

**Applications:**
- Earnings prediction using regression models  
- Risk scoring using weighted factors  
- Anomaly detection for fraud prevention  

**Data sources:**
- Weather APIs  
- Simulated traffic and order data  
- Platform activity signals  

---

## 8. System Workflow

1. Delivery partner registers and selects operating zone  
2. System calculates weekly premium  
3. Partner activates coverage  
4. Real-time monitoring tracks conditions and earnings  
5. Disruption leads to income drop detection  
6. Claim is triggered automatically  
7. Payout is processed instantly  

---

## 9. Technology Stack

- **Frontend:** Android application

- **Database:** MongoDB  

**APIs:**
- Weather integration  
- Simulated platform and traffic data

RakshAI provides a structured and responsive safety layer for delivery partners operating in uncertain environments.

The platform enables:
- Protection against short-term income disruptions  
- Personalised and fair pricing  
- Automated and fast payouts  
- Increased trust through transparency  

RakshAI aligns financial protection with the realities of gig work and supports a workforce that operates without traditional safeguards.
